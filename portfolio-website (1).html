About Me

Creating Stories That Matter

Creative filmmaker, screenwriter, and director experienced in guiding projects from concept through post-production. I combine sharp storytelling instincts with four years of high-pressure coordination at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, which translates seamlessly to production management.

I'm passionate about genre-blending stories that amplify marginalized voices and spark conversation. My background in both healthcare coordination and creative production gives me a unique perspective on managing teams and delivering compelling narratives.

When I'm not behind the camera or writing, you might find me volunteering at the Outfest LGBTQ+ Film Festival or drawing on my experience as a former punk-band drummer to create rhythmic, dynamic edits.
